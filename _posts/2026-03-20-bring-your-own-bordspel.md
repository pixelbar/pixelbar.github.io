On May 16 Pixelbar turns into a battlefield of dice, cards, and questionable alliances.

This evening is all about board games. Whether you're here to slay dragons, betray your friends, conquer kingdoms, or rage over a brutal game of Mensch ärgere dich nicht — there's a seat at the table.

Expect clever strategies, dramatic victories, and the occasional spectacular defeat. Bring your favorite game, join one of the tables, or jump into something completely new.

Projects can stay at home… unless your project is the board game.

Everyone is welcome, from casual players to hardcore strategists.

**Location**  
We are at Schiemond 20, 3024 EE Rotterdam. This is inside "De Kroon", follow the signs for K14.  
If you're coming with public transportation: you can go to Tram stop Schiemond or Metro Station Delfshaven and then walk towards the harbor.  
To access the space you need to go up a set stairs, we sadly don’t have an elevator.
